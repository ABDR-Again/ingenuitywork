Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe with automation? expand_more

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and secure authentication methods (OAuth 2.0). We design workflows that pass data through without storing it on our servers, ensuring your sensitive information remains within your own ecosystem.

What if I need to change the process later? expand_more

Our systems are modular by design. Unlike rigid custom software, building with tools like Make/Zapier allows for flexibility. We can easily adjust logic branches or add new steps as your business evolves, or we can train your team to make minor updates.

Do I need to pay for software subscriptions? expand_more

Yes, most automation platforms like Zapier or Make have their own subscription costs based on usage. During our consultation, we will estimate these costs for you to ensure the ROI makes sense for your business.

Can you automate legacy desktop software? expand_more

It depends. If the software has an API or database access, yes. If not, we can sometimes use RPA (Robotic Process Automation) tools, but we specialize in modern cloud-based integrations for maximum reliability.
