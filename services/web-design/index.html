Frequently Asked Questions

Do you use pre-made templates? expand_more

We primarily focus on custom themes to ensure uniqueness and performance. However, if you're on a tight budget, we can customize a premium theme to match your brand guidelines perfectly.

Will I be able to edit the website myself? expand_more

Absolutely. WordPress is user-friendly. We use page builders like Elementor or Gutenberg blocks, making it easy for you to edit text and images without touching code. We also provide a training session.

What do I need to provide to get started? expand_more

We'll need your logo, brand guidelines (colors, fonts), and the content for your pages (text and images). If you don't have content yet, we can use placeholders or offer copywriting services as an add-on.

Is hosting and domain included? expand_more

No, the domain and hosting are not included in the development fee. However, we can guide you in purchasing the right plan and will handle the setup and configuration on your chosen server.
