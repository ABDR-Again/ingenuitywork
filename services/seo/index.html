Frequently Asked Questions

Why does SEO take time? expand_more

SEO is a compound effect game. Google needs time to crawl, index, and trust your new content and backlinks. While some fixes yield quick wins, sustainable ranking growth typically builds momentum over 3-6 months.

Do you guarantee #1 rankings? expand_more

No ethical SEO agency can guarantee a #1 spot because search algorithms are third-party systems we don't control. We guarantee best practices, transparent work, and a strategy proven to increase visibility and traffic.

What is the difference between On-Page and Off-Page SEO? expand_more

On-Page SEO involves optimizing elements on your website (content, speed, tags), while Off-Page SEO focuses on building authority externally, primarily through backlinks and social signals.

Do I need SEO if I'm running ads? expand_more

Yes. Ads stop working the moment you stop paying. SEO builds long-term organic equity that continues to bring in leads without a per-click cost, lowering your overall customer acquisition cost.

How do I track progress? expand_more

We provide a live dashboard where you can see your keyword rankings, organic traffic growth, and conversion metrics in real-time. We also send a detailed PDF report every month.
